Signature Recognition

Developed two neural networks with custom loss functions. Trained one on genuine signatures and the other on genuine as well as skilled forgeries. Used these models to extract discriminative features from signature images. Trained XGBoost classifiers to classify between signatures based on features extracted from both the models. Stacked the two classifiers to better learn the difference between the genuine and forged images. The project was made for the Axis AI Challenge Grand Finale.
